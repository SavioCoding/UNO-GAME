The UNO Game -- Game Instruction
Set Up
  • The game is for 2 players.
  • Every player starts with seven cards, and they are dealt face down.
  • The rest of the cards are placed in the Draw Pile.
  • There should be a Discard Pile Next to the Draw pile for the players to play cards.
  • The top card should be placed in the Discard Pile, and the game begins!
  • You Can Now Play the UNO GAME!!
How to Play?
  • You should match the card in the DISCARD pile.
  • You can match the card with same color, number, or symbols.
  • You can play the Wild Card and Wild Draw Four Card on any color
  • If you cannot play a card, draw 1 card.
  • You can only play 1 card at a time.
  • When a player plays their last card, the game is over.
